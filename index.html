A roof replacement in Newark, Ohio typically costs between $8,000 and $20,000 for an average-sized home, depending on roof size, pitch, and materials. If your roof is under 15 years old with isolated damage, repair is usually the right call. If it's 20+ years old, shows widespread granule loss, has multiple leaks, or took significant storm damage, full replacement is typically more cost-effective. We provide free inspections throughout Licking County and give you an honest recommendation either way.
Most Ohio homeowner's insurance policies cover sudden storm damage from hail, high winds, and falling debris. Normal wear and aging is typically not covered. After a storm, document damage quickly and contact your insurer promptly — there are filing deadlines. Newark Elite Roofing assists with damage documentation and works with all major insurance carriers.
