If your roof is under 15 years old with isolated damage, repair is usually the right call. Most Ohio homeowner's policies cover sudden storm damage from hail, high winds, and falling debris — but there are filing deadlines, and normal wear is not covered. After a storm, document damage with photos immediately. Newark Elite Roofing works with all major insurance carriers and assists with full claim documentation.
